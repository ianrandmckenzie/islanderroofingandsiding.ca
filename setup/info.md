# Islander Roofing and Siding

## The Business
**Business Name:** Islander Roofing and Siding
**Location:** Courtenay / Comox (Mid-Vancouver Island), BC
**Core Specialty:** High-performance, climate-resilient roofing and siding systems.
**Unique Value Proposition:** Unlike general contractors, this business operates as a technical authority on "Island-Standard" exteriors. It leverages deep data and 2026 BC Building Code expertise to provide solutions specifically engineered for the Mid-Island’s unique environmental stressors—including 100km/h wind loads, salt-air corrosion, and extreme humidity.
**Brand Identity:** Authoritative, rugged, and technically proficient. The aesthetic is modern and high-end, utilizing a "protection-first" communication style and a sophisticated palette of deep reds (#BF403A) and industrial grays.
**Target Audience:** Informed homeowners in micro-markets like Crown Isle and the Comox Peninsula who prioritize long-term durability and engineering over the lowest bid.

## Design Caveat
Since the palette is quite heavy on red (primary, secondary, tertiary), use the tertiary (#914139) sparingly for "Trust" elements like borders, icons, or text-links within gray blocks. This keeps the site looking like a premium technical service rather than a fast-food brand.
