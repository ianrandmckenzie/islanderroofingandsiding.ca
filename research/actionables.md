# How to use the docs in this folder

## 1. Courtenay Roofing & Siding Research.md

Extract "Hyper-Local" Intelligence
Instead of asking broad questions, use Deep Research to identify the specific technical requirements and psychological triggers of North Island homeowners.

Try this prompt in the Research Tool:

"Research the top 5 consumer complaints for roofing and siding contractors in Courtenay and Comox, BC. Identify specific weather-related pain points (e.g., salt air corrosion, moss growth, wind uplift) unique to the Comox Peninsula. Provide a list of the top 10 high-intent long-tail keywords for 'Roofing Courtenay' that have low competition but high local relevance in 2026."

✅ Prompt complete, see: Courtenay Roofing & Siding Research.md

Why this works: You’ll get data on things like "moss-resistant shingles for Comox" or "salt-grade metal siding for coastal homes." You can then build dedicated landing pages for these exact problems.

## 2. Courtenay Comox Neighborhood Housing.md

Technical SEO on a Budget
Since you can code, you don’t need to pay for expensive SEO suites. Use Deep Research to find the "Technical Gaps" your competitors (who likely use basic Wix/Squarespace sites) are missing.

The "Programmatic Neighborhood" Strategy
The Comox Valley is a collection of distinct micro-markets (Crown Isle, the Comox Peninsula, Cumberland, Royston).

Prompt: "List all distinct residential neighborhoods in Courtenay and Comox. For each, identify the dominant housing style (e.g., 90s stucco, new modern farmhouse) and the likely age of their current roofs."

✅ Prompt complete, see: Courtenay Comox Neighborhood Housing.md

Execution: Create a page for every neighborhood (e.g., yoursite.com/roofing/crown-isle). Use your technical skill to inject Local Business Schema (JSON-LD) that specifies these service areas. This "hyper-local" targeting is an SEO cheat code for small businesses.

## 3. Contractor Website Conversion Strategies.md

Conversion Optimization (2026 Standards)
In 2026, a "Contact Us" form isn't enough. Use Deep Research to see what high-end service businesses are doing to reduce friction.

Prompt for the tool:

"Analyze high-converting service-based websites in 2026. What are the current best practices for 'micro-interactions' and 'AI-driven personalization' for local contractors? Specifically, look for interactive quote builders and visual project galleries."

✅ Prompt complete, see: Contractor Website Conversion Strategies.md

Technical Implementation:

Interactive Estimator: Instead of a form, build a "Roof Cost Calculator." It doesn't have to be perfect; it just needs to provide a range in exchange for an email.

Proof of Competence: Don't just show a finished roof. Use a "Weather-Tech" section explaining why you use specific fasteners or underlayment for Island conditions (High-wind ratings and 2026 BC Building Code compliance).
